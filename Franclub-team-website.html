Our Vision & Mindset

At The Franclub, we believe in the power of diverse perspectives and niche interests to drive innovation. As Washington and Lee sophomores, we combine our unique academic backgrounds to create solutions that others might overlook.

Our collective mindset to succeed drives everything we do. We recognize that the intersection of political science, accounting, philosophy, studio art, mathematics, and physics creates a unique vantage point from which to approach challenges.

We're committed to developing ideas that are not only successful but also meaningful and impactful in today's rapidly changing world.
